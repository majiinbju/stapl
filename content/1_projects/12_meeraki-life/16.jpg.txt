Caption: Optimizing the contour of the land, the clubhouse is located on the slope towards the centre. This is also along the sightlines of the plot. Using the theory of cut and fill to balance the impact of building and trying to retain the natural topography of land by terracing. Given these contours; the entry level of the clubhouse has a sunken pool while the roof of the clubhouse is covered by a landscaped lawn that meets the internal road above. A scooped out courtyard with a naturally forming waterfall become the central focal point of the form finish clubhouse.
